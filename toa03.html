Select the Stylesheet

There are five stylesheets that you may select from. Each one will change the coloring, fonts, and even the layout of the page.You can change the layout of this page by selecting different stylesheets in the menu, or by selecting one of the following links:
Stylesheet 1, Stylesheet 2, Stylesheet 3, Stylesheet 4, Stylesheet 5.

Instructions

Stylesheet 1: Changes the colors of text, adds a background image, and makes the page dynamically center with a resizing of the page. It also puts all the paragraphs in text boxes.
Stylesheet 2: Changes the color, style, placement, and size of text. It makes the background black. It also adds a line over the text, and it adds a colored block to the list.
Stylesheet 3: Changes the color of the background and text. The title has an animation that causes the color to change on an infinite loop. There are 2 different types of borders included, both different colors. The Style Sheet menu is floating to the left, and the instructions are floating left. The Grading Sidebar clears both of these elements.
Stylesheet 4: This stylesheet is all about animation. The title heading moves across the screen, while the other h1 headings change font-size. The border for the grading section also resizes on it's own. There are many different font colors, weights, and families. h3 has a variant of small-caps. The two text paragraphs have a double border, and the links have the underline removed.
Stylesheet 5: The use of hover is on full display in this stylesheet. Hovering over paragraphs, headings, or the bulleted list will yield interesting results and transitions are used to make it all look seamless. Also, text shadows are used on headings.
